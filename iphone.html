iPhone XR

The fuselage is equipped with a 7-nm A12 bionic chip and uses TrueDepth camera to support Face ID.

The back uses a 12-megapixel single camera. With the A12 chip, the iPhone XR supports portrait mode.

The iPhone XR has a 6.1-inch LCD screen with a resolution of 1792 x 828 and 326 PPi. Apple has named the screen Liquid Retina. By contrast, the phone will not support 3D Touch, but will support click wake-up. In terms of storage configuration, the machine has three versions: 64GB, 128GB and 256GB.

The iPhone XR carries the "nerve engine" AI processing. The iPhone XR will be equipped with a rear single camera, similar to the iPhone 8. The front-end camera of the iPhone XR supports TrueDepth depth sensing technology, 7 megapixel sensors, Face ID and self-timer in portrait mode, as well as Animoji and Mooji.
